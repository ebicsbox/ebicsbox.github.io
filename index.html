We took the pain out of EBICS


What is EBICS?

The Electronic Banking Internet Communication Standard (short: EBICS) is a new standard for payment transfer in the internet which is accepted by many banks in Germany. EBICS is the successor (after a transition) of the standard BCS-FTAM, which has no internet protocol.

What is the EBICS::BOX?

The EBICS::Box is a complete solution for the simple integration of financial transactions in existing systems. Every task type is supported. The EBICS:Box offers support for multiple banks and every bank that has an EBICS access.

Features of the EBICS::Box

Transactions

We support SEPA transactions

Debits

Depending on your use, COR1, CORE oder B2B debits are possible.

Monitoring of transactions

Your account movements get retrieved automatically and can be searched through easily.

Webhooks

We transfer all important transactions and events via Webhooks to your application.
